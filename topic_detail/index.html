TOKYO, June 2, 2025 – JCB Co., Ltd., Japan’s only global payment brand, is proud to announce that its exclusive cardmember mobile app, the MyJCB App, has been honored with the Gold Award in the Mobile Technologies, Applications, and Software Design category at the A’ Design Award & Competition.

This recognition follows the app’s recent success at the iF DESIGN AWARD 2025, making yet another significant milestone for the MyJCB App. The Gold Award is bestowed upon designs that place within the top 3% of all submissions, reflecting an exceptional standard of design excellence.

The app’s service design and UI/UX design were developed in collaboration with FOURDIGIT Inc.
